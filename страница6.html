New installation steps for every AOSP ROM
Clean flash, this will erase everything inside your storage

1. Format data
2. Reboot recovery
3. Wipe all except OTG and SDCard partitions
4. Flash FirmwareQUpdater (for Android 10) or FirmwareR1Updater (for android 11)
5. Flash ROM
6. Reboot recovery
(Optional step. Resize system if you want to have more space in your system partition)
7. Flash any GApps (if they weren't already included) and/or mods (like magisk)
Note: This will also work without qfil rom, if you skip any of these flashing steps you will get a bootloader error and you will have to flash qfil rom or flash vbmeta from the group note #bootloaderLoop-fix

Do not flash FirmwareR1Updater on a10 ROMs or you will probably get audio policy bugs, specially with things like Bluetooth earphones where they will not work
